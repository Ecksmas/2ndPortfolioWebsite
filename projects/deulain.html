A prototype of a product that is supposed to enable short-wave-communication between people in a short radius to ultimately take the conversation offline instead. Inspired by PictoChat and other messaging apps.

In its current form, the project is still waiting on the Pico 2 to be delivered. After that, the project will start with creating a menu system to later on add the communication capabilities of the prototype.
